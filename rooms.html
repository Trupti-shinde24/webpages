ROOMS AND SUITES



Our rooms and suites reflect a thoughtful harmony of Mewar inspired motifs, elegant furnishings and modern technologies.



Our Premier Rooms.


A premier experience of Oberoi hospitality.
Uninterrupted garden views are best enjoyed from the cushioned bay window seat in these stunning rooms with 55.74 square metres interiors. Decorated in a natural palette, with vibrant touches of colour and traditional Indian patterns, the spacious interiors include a king sized bed and a marble ensuite bathroom with views of a private sun dappled courtyard.

Our Premier Rooms with Pool View.


Matchless comfort with an authentic vibe.
These spacious rooms with 55.74 square metres interiors, have been carefully designed for your utmost comfort, with a king size bed and a marble bathroom with private courtyard views. Our Luxury Suites with Private Pool.


Your own private space to relax and unwind.
Enjoy garden views and exquisite luxury in our suites with 106.83 square metres interiors, from the elegant living room to the king size bedroom, ensuite bathroom, dining room, powder room and private courtyard. Our Kohinoor Suite with Private Pool.


Our crowning glory, especially for you.
With its gold domed ceilings, mirror mosaics and hand carved marble privacy screens, the Kohinoor Suite with 246.19 square metres interiors, is a rarity. A master bedroom and twin bedroom, a spectacular dining room, living room and private pool occupy a tasteful 246.2 square metres. All the very latest technology, including three television sets, bring a touch of modernity to this true classic.
